Should I Start my own Social Media?

The answer is simple... YES. Not so you can get free tools or paid sponsorship (although they are nice perks). It's about building a personal brand, to showcase your abilities and skills and network with people within your sector/niche.

Tips to get Started:

First figure out what you want to talk about, and why you are talking about it. It has to be something your passionate about, you want to spread awareness or you just want to give advice, be really specific on your WHY this will be the driving force.

What type of content are you going to post will depend on the platform, there are only 3 ways in which you can communicate to engage with your audience, written word, video & audio now depending if you want to include your face or not will depend what path you go down. Written Word - Medium Blogs, LinkedIn, WordPress Video - YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram Audio - Soundcloud, Stitcher, Google Voice, iTunes (Podcast)

How to get noticed is another thing you have to understand the mechanics of, you either leverage your Time or Money. If you've got Money you can use Google/Facebook Ads to push your content to your desired audience, otherwise if you have time you need to start creating and create a lot of content in as many places as you can to gain the most exposure to every eyeball you can.
